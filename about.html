A bit of our origin
Our Story

1976

began...

HAMISH ANDERSON STARTED IN THE CONSTRUCTION INDUSTRY AS A FINISH CARPENTER IN 1976 WHERE HE FOCUSED MAINLY ON COMMERICAL CONSTRUCTION. OVER THE NEXT 10 YEARS HE GAINED A VAST KNOWLEDGE OF UNIQUE TYPES OF CONSTRUCTION AS WELL AS A WORK ETHIC THAT WAS CLIENT CENTERED AND METICULOUS - AS A FINISH CARPENTER BY TRADE, THE DETAILS MATTERED TO HIM.

1990

The First Company

IN AN EFFORT TO MAKE A LIFE FOR HIS GROWING FAMILY, HAMISH ANDERSON FOUNDED HIS FIRST COMPANY IN 1990 WITH THE INCORPORATION OF NOVELTY HOMES.

1992

A new name

AFTER HAVING COMPLETED A HANDFUL OF SUCCESSFUL PROJECTS, NOVELTY HOMES WAS AMENDED TO HAMISH ANDERSON CUSTOM HOMES IN OCTOBER OF 1992, A NAME IT HOLDS TO THIS DAY.

2010

A Family Business

IT HAS ALWAYS BEEN HAMISH’S DESIRE TO SHARE HIS LOVE OF CONSTRUCTION WITH HIS FAMILY, AND IN 2010, HAMISH HIRED HIS FIRST SON COLIN INTO THE BUSINESS, WHERE HE CURRENTLY WORKS AS ITS SENIOR CONSTRUCTION MANAGER. SEVERAL YEARS LATER, HAMISH’S SECOND SON JARED ALSO JOINED THE TEAM TO ASSIST WITH THE COMPANY’S GROWING BUSINESS.

2011

Best Builder

IN 2011, HAMISH ANDERSON CUSTOM HOMES WAS VOTED “BEST BUILDER” BY 425 MAGAZINE. 425 MAGAZINE HONORED OUR EFFORTS REMARKING: “TAILORING LIVING SPACES TO AN INDIVIDUAL OR FAMILY’S UNIQUE NEED IS HAMISH ANDERSON’S PASSION. ATTENTION TO DETAIL, QUALITY MATERIALS AND CONSTRUCTION, AND 32 YEARS IN THE BUSINESS PUT THIS HIGH-END HOME BUILDER ON THE TOP RUNG.”

2014

New Projects

HAMISH ANDERSON CUSTOM HOMES HAS GROWN TO 9 EMPLOYEES WITH PROJECTS SPANNING THROUGHOUT THE GREATER SEATTLE AREA AND EASTSIDE.
